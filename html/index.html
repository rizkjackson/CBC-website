Computational Biology Core (CBC)
We are here to help you advance your research!

Welcome to the UCSF Computational Biology Core.

Our objective is to provide state-of-the-art computational biology support for cancer-related research at UCSF. We have expertise in genetics, bioinformatics, computer science and statistics with long-term experience in high-throughput sequencing, microarrays, and other genomic and proteomic technologies. Our activities include, but are not limited to, scientific consultation, study design, data analysis, software development and grant and manuscript assistance.

Our resources are prioritized toward members of the UCSF Helen Diller Family Comprehensive Cancer Center, but as time permits, we provide support to outside investigators. If you believe we can be of help to you, feel free to drop by one of our offices or grab one of us in the hallway. You can of course schedule a meeting by email or phone. Initial discussions are always free.
